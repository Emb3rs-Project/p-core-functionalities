What is an Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) unit?

An Organic Rankine Cycle – ORC - is a thermodynamic cycle that converts heat to electricity. ORC units utilize a source of heat, usually excess heat, and produce electricity, either for self-consumption or to sell to the grid (for more information check EMB3Rs deliverable D.3.3).

What can you obtain from this report?

The simulation assesses for a unique and multiples streams, the amount of recoverable excess heat, and its conversion to electricity by implementing an ORC unit. The output of this simulation are the 3 best ORC designs in terms of lowest specific cost of electrical generation (€/kWh).

What is the report structure?

It is presented:

  • A table with the streams that were assessed. Only streams whose supply temperature is larger than the ORC's evaporator temperature are analyzed
  • An overview on the best 3 designed ORC solutions in electrical generation specific cost
  • A detailed technical analysis on each solution designed
  • A detailed economic analysis on each solution designed
